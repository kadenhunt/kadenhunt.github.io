Welcome — why CS+UX matters in my work

Dec 11, 2025 · Back to Blog

I’m Kaden — a design-minded CS student who cares a lot about the “in-between” decisions: the small interface choices, the performance tradeoffs, and the details that make a product feel intentional.

When I say CS+UX, I’m talking about building experiences that are:

  • Clear: users understand what’s happening and what to do next.
  • Reliable: the UI behaves consistently across devices and browsers.
  • Fast: responsive by design, not by accident.
  • Accessible: usable with keyboard, screen readers, and real-world constraints.

This blog will be where I share short breakdowns of projects, UI decisions, and what I’m learning. If you’re reading this, thanks for stopping by.
