About Me

I’m a Computer Science student at Western Kentucky University with a strong focus on User Experience, graduating Spring 2026.

I build products where engineering and design meet — pairing clean UI with thoughtful UX and code that’s easy to run, test, and maintain. My recent work includes Android apps, Docker-based microservices, CI-driven test automation, and data-informed technical write-ups.

My Approach

I take a user-first, evidence-driven approach: understand the problem, prototype quickly, validate with real feedback, then ship. I care deeply about clear requirements, strong communication, and documentation that actually helps teams move faster.

Long-Term Goal

Long-term, I want to build a website design and development consulting studio — partnering with companies to modernize their web presence, run collaborative working sessions, and provide end-to-end support from launch to long-term maintenance.
